Who Am I ?

Who am I ? A simple question with an the most difficult answer. I am many things, professionally a techie, employed as a Sr Technical Architect specializing in the field of UI. On the personal front I am a father, a husband and trying my best in being the best friend of the wonderful woman I married. But one thing I am passionate about the most is travelling by road to remote destinations, and doing landscape photography. Also I am a foodie (although I have been dethroned by my wife in this area), who loves to experiment with different cuisines.

A Techie

I started my career as a Java + Python + Smalltalk developer (I wanted to be pursue microbioly or biotechnology but thats a different story) in 1998. However after the first dotcom collapse in 2000-2001 I switched to UI technology as it seemed to be the right choice at the time. Thus I started my UI journey working as a Flash developer and also pursued my MSC in Multimedia Information System from the University of Portsmouth. After completing my postgraduation and a brief stint with the Department of Creative Technologies of the University I decided to come back to India and restart my professional career.

During my tenure I have been fortunate enough to provide services to many esteemed clients such as Peerson Press, Mcgraw Hill, Siemens, IHG, Ticketmaster, Taj Hotels, Dunkin, Unilever and many more.

Apart from delivering projects I have also been actively involved in training and mentoring tech enthusiasts, writing white papers etc. However one thing that I have enjoyed the most is working on empowering ideas and groundbreaking thought process within the orgnizations that I have worked for. I have been hailed as a visionary and a thought evangelist for providing ideas and concepts to work upon and to this day many people from my current to past organizations have been keeping in touch with me to discuss on groundbreaking thoughts. Some of the POCs and ideas that I have been a part of are:

  • Data visualization using HTML Canvas and Particle sytem.
  • Website builder that was powered by google webspeech API.
  • Home automation system using Nodejs and Arduino circuit.
  • A module builder based on JS modular pattern using Grunt.
  • Proximity sensor and face recognition algorithm coupled with HTML (Canvas and SVG) that displayed the user's information on smart screen.
  • Simulations involving conversation between two individuals where the system would track the flow of the conversation and provide its assessment to the user based on the conversation path followed by the user.
  • Simulation depicting the day in the life of a professional and the system's assessment on the different course of action taken by the individual on each task.

What drives me ?

UI is a field that has always fascinated me, I have never got bored and I will not get bored of it because even after being with the field for 19 years I have always found something new to learn. I never felt that I have learned enough and there is nothing more left, the challenges have always helped me grow and have instilled more confidence in me.

Over the years I have also been blessed to be a part of various areas of technology, be it e-Learning, web development, RIA, Simulations, Data visualization and so on. I feel UI is one area which always present some ground breaking methodology as you constantly need to think how to present visually rich information to the end user.

A Foodie

Everyone eats to live but I live to eat. Since my childhood I was have always been fascinated with trying anything that catches my attention. At the restaurant I always used to look for items in the menu that I never heard of before or anything that sounded unusual. After many successful and disastrous tries I was able to develop a taste for many different cuisnines, moreover when my wife and I first met I made sure she had the foodie gene in her (yes being a foodie is that important for me) and boy the way she has exceeded my expectation, she has become really methodical and her take on technicalities are amazing. Before meeting her I just saw food and used to gobble it but now I regularly get lessons on what's amuse bouche, brisket etc.

What drives me ?

Needless to say that curiosity always drives me in trying something new whenever I get the opportuinity. I always like to eat different kind of cuisines and remember them. Moreover while travelling I always make sure that I eat local food which in turn helps in widening my knowledge when it comes to food.

A Traveller

When I am not working I am busy planning my next destination. Traveling by road and exploring different destinations keeps my sanity in check (I turn green if I dont get to travel for even few months!). I am a part of a group of fellow travelling enthusiasts who specialize in covering unforgiving and remote landscapes, I have been fortunate enough to do a couple of roadtrips with them. However I also do solo trips when the members of the group are not travelling (More on my travel experiences are covered in my travelog).

What drives me ?

The passion and the excitement to see new landscapes, meeting new people, learning about their culture, eating what the locals eat and of course sharing my experiences always gives me the boost to get behind the wheels and drive. It is the reason that keeps my sanity in check, I can literally go on driving for hours and you would literally need a crowbar to remove me from the driving seat .
