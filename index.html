Building Bridges

Hello World!

Welcome to my Planet! This space is an effort to make my personalised digital presence to be served under one medium. Although we have various social mediums (Facebook to be the most popular of all), however I feel that all that information from one source (in this case the source would be me) is all scattered, therefore I wanted to set up my own space where I can spend some time penning down my thoughts, my experiences etc. For instance whenever I travel to any place or read about any social issue, myriad thoughts and ideas burst into my head which I pen down in my notebook but never can I find the appropriate digital medium to echo those thoughts online. That’s when I decided to bring out subzworld, a medium to serve exactly what I want (and of course being digital its easier to manage).

While you are here please feel free to checkout the blog section. I have categorized my blogs according to my travel sagas and my foodie experience, I would keep on updating my blogs as regularly as possible.

I hope you enjoy your stay, oh! while you’re here, please do leave your comment and feedback as it will be really valuable for me as a platform to learn more.
